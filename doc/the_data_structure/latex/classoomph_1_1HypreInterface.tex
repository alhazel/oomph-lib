\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
An interface class to the suite of Hypre solvers and preconditioners to allow use of\+: 

Boomer\+AMG (AMG), \mbox{\hyperlink{classoomph_1_1CG}{CG}}, \mbox{\hyperlink{classoomph_1_1GMRES}{GMRES}} or \mbox{\hyperlink{classoomph_1_1BiCGStab}{Bi\+CGStab}}, Euclid (ILU) or Para\+Sails (Approximate inverse)

Hypre\textquotesingle{}s Krylov subspace solvers (\mbox{\hyperlink{classoomph_1_1CG}{CG}}, \mbox{\hyperlink{classoomph_1_1GMRES}{GMRES}} and \mbox{\hyperlink{classoomph_1_1BiCGStab}{Bi\+CGStab}}) may be preconditioned using\+: \begin{DoxyVerb}    BoomerAMG, Euclid or ParaSails
\end{DoxyVerb}
